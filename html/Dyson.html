MY ROLE AND RESPONSIBILITIES

A big challenge I experienced was cross team collaboration. I was placed in the design team and collaborated with people from the test team, technical development team, commercials, and also the lab professionals. Much of this interaction has to be initiated on my own part in order to learn as much as possible and do as much in a short span of 10 weeks.

I also had to learn lots of prototyping and machine skills in order to build my test rig.

Most importantly, I had to be flexible, adaptable and decisive. It was an iterative process to design the test rig, because many of the initial designs failed. I learnt to try, fail, try again, and eventually succeed.
