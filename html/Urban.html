MY ROLE AND RESPONSIBILITIES

As I was the only one who had Design and Architecture 3D modelling (Revit) experience, I was leading most of the design decisions and also the technical modelling of the site. During the brainstorming session, I took everyone's individual Masterplan and further developed them with the team, combining them to form the Final Plan, The Turtle. Urban design requires a lot of thoughtful thinking, and we tried to make the best experience for the residents and visitors of our site, increasing interconnectivity and urban interactions.
